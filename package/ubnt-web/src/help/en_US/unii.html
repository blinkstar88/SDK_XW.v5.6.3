Revised UNII Rules

For more information about revised UNII rules activation, please visit www.ubnt.com/fcclabelrequest. From here you can request a unique activation code for your company.

Important: Activating revised UNII rules will change EIRP limits and will remove 5 MHz and 8 MHz channel widths. Be careful before activating new rules on longer distance links. After activation device will be restarted to take an effect.
